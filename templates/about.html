Our Story

Baked with Love,

Prajakta's kitchen is where passion meets purpose — homemade cupcakes crafted with real ingredients, real care, and a whole lot of heart, one sweet bite at a time.

A Legacy, Baked Fresh

From Her Kitchen,
With Love

Prajakta is a devoted wife, a talented beautician, and now a passionate home baker. Between her beauty clients and caring for her home, she found her truest joy in the kitchen — baking cupcakes that made everyone around her smile a little wider.

What began as a quiet way to support her family and stand by her husband has blossomed into something beautiful. Every cupcake she bakes carries the same warmth she puts into everything she does — made fresh, made from scratch, made with purpose.
